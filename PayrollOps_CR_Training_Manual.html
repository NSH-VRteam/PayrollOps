03 / Boundaries
Very important

What PayrollOps is not

Do not position PayrollOps as

  • Full payroll outsourcing
  • Statutory filing service
  • CA replacement
  • Employer of record
  • Free tool or mandatory service
  • Global payroll company
  • A guarantee of error-free payroll

Correct positioning

  • Payroll operations support service
  • Supports HR and finance teams
  • Improves setup, dashboards, payslips, reports
  • Client still owns payroll approval and decisions
  • PayrollOps makes the process smoother
Your HR/finance team keeps control. PayrollOps helps structure and support the workflow.

05 / Why It Exists
Client pain

Payroll problems CRs should recognize

Manual process pain

  • Payroll still happens in Excel
  • HR spends too much time checking data manually
  • Attendance, LOP, revisions, and deductions are manually reconciled
  • Payroll errors are found too late

Visibility and support pain

  • CA output gives no dashboard visibility
  • Payslip format is not professional or customized
  • Reports are not in management format
  • Software support is slow or configuration is confusing

When you hear pain, move to callback

Do not try to solve payroll. Collect the right details and hand over the lead.

12 / Data Safety
Confidentiality rule

Never ask for payroll files or employee data

Do not ask for

  • Salary sheet or employee salary data
  • PAN, Aadhaar, bank details
  • PF/ESI numbers
  • Employee master file
  • Attendance file
  • Payroll register or tax declaration files
  • Payslip samples before formal discussion

Collect only lead details

  • Company name and property
  • Contact person and role
  • Phone / WhatsApp and work email
  • Approximate employee count
  • Current payroll method
  • Main challenge
  • Permission for callback
Data safetyLead details only

13 / Lead Grading
Qualification score

Classify every lead clearly

A-Grade

50-150 employees, right contact identified, clear pain, wants callback, decision-maker or influencer available.

Action: Send form and schedule PayrollOps callback.

B-Grade

20-50 employees or growing, current method unclear, interested but not urgent, right person not available yet.

Action: Send form and follow up once.

C-Grade

No clear pain, casually curious, employee asked but HR contact unknown, very small team, not ready.

Action: Share brochure only.

D-Grade

No interest, already satisfied, wants full outsourcing/statutory filing only, wants free tool, or highly complex scope.

Action: Do not push. Mark future/not suitable.

20 / Sensitive Questions
Handle carefully

Sensitive questions require safe answers

Do you run payroll for us?

PayrollOps supports payroll setup, dashboards, payslips, reports, and workflow or technical issues. Your HR or finance team continues to review, approve, and roll payroll. Our PayrollOps team can explain the exact scope clearly.

Do you handle PF/ESI/TDS?

Our PayrollOps team will clarify the service scope properly. CR team does not confirm statutory filing ownership. PayrollOps mainly supports setup, configuration, dashboards, reports, payslips, and workflow support.

Can we send salary data now?

Please do not share salary data at this stage. First, our PayrollOps team will schedule a discussion, understand your requirement, and explain the secure process.

Sensitive questionsDo not overpromise

21 / Price Questions
Pricing and free questions

Do not invent pricing. Escalate politely.

Is this free?

The first enquiry or discussion can be arranged by our team. The PayrollOps service itself depends on the scope, company size, setup effort, and support requirement. Our PayrollOps team will explain the details.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on your current payroll process, employee count, reports, payslip requirements, and support needs. Our PayrollOps team will first understand your setup and then share the suitable package.

We are a small team

No problem. PayrollOps is more useful for growing teams where payroll is becoming difficult to manage. I can still arrange a discussion if your team expects to grow or if payroll is already taking too much time.

23 / Never Say
Red lines

CR must never say these lines

  • We will run your payroll completely.
  • We will handle all statutory compliance.
  • We will replace your CA.
  • We will replace Zoho/Keka/greytHR.
  • We guarantee no payroll errors.
  • This is the best payroll software.
  • This is free or compulsory.
  • Send your salary sheet to me.
  • Share employee details with me.
  • We will file PF/ESI/TDS for you.
  • We will make you compliant automatically.
  • We can support all countries.
  • We can support any company size immediately.
Wrong promises can create legal, trust, and delivery issues.
